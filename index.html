... and Jumping Higher

  • Gold medal high jump and pole vault heights have steadily increased over time
  • For instance, William Hoyt's 1896 pole vault of 3.3 meters was nearly doubled by Thiago Da Silva's 2016 vault of 6.03 meters.
  • The increase in jumping/vaulting height persists across both men and women
  • Seemingly, the rate of improvement is slowing down
