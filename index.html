Usain Bolt's Dominance

Over the course of his career, usain bolt has...

  • Received gold in the 100m and 200m in three consecutive Olympics - in 2008, 2012, and 2016
  • Won the 200m by a 0.66 second margin - the largest in Olympic history
  • Earned 8 Olympic and 11 World Championship gold medals

A Little More on Track and Field

Before answering questions like the ones raised about Bolt, it's important to know a bit about track and field. Time to take a deeper dive into the sport! With 44 events between men and women, track and field dominates the Olympics. Not only do track and field competitors make up the largest share of athletes at the games, but they also vie for the most medals of any sport. Events span a wide array of skill sets â€” from running fast to throwing far. Millions of eyes tune in to watch the best athletes in the world run, jump, and throw every four years. It truly is a centerpiece of the Olympics!

... and Jumping Higher

What do we see?

  • Gold medal high jump and pole vault heights have steadily increased over time
  • For instance, William Hoyt's 1896 pole vault of 3.3 meters was nearly doubled by Thiago Da Silva's 2016 vault of 6.03 meters
  • The increase in jumping/vaulting height persists across both men and women
  • Seemingly, the rate of improvement is slowing down
